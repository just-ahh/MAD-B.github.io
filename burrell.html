Log Carving Game

Players within this game must use their hand and arm to cut through air to carve the virtual wood.

Using the empty space in front of the player as the game area gives a more high-tech experience than using traditional controls.

Once the player has carved the wood to the desired shape, the game will use the shape to create a table or other traditional wooden furniture.

Pottery Spin Game

This exhibit allows players to spin a bowl to display different patterns onto it.

The bowl has a projector above it to show different shapes and patterns upon the bowl.

As the player spins faster or in a different direction, the pattern changes creating a unique piece.
