Benefits

Why should you care about there being museum video games for adults?

There are many reasons of the benefits of using video games for learning, a few of them are listed below:

Motivation

  • Video games allow players to feel a sense of agency and the ability to make decisions, but with a limited choices due to the games scope.
  • User driven narratives are more engaging – players are more motivated to take in information when they are given the choice to.
  • Achievements – Players have more motivation to keep playing to unlock all the outcomes or get the best score.


Gamification

  • Video games are everywhere and can be used to help learning in all ages.
  • With internet use growing, with 66% of people around the world online, why should this style of games not be for adults?


Learning Pyramid

  • Learning Pyramid shows example of how effective different learning styles are.
  • We remember 20% from an audiovisual experience, 30% from a demonstration and 75% from practicing
  • We only remember 10% of what we read, making video games a more effective method to learn information.
  • The more engaging the exhibit, the more people will learn.
