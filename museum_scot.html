Key Information

Free Entry.

10 minute walk from Edinburgh Waverley train station.

Racing Game

2 Players race against each other to complete as many laps as possible within a time limit.

The players are on a fixed track and must utilise acceleration and breaking to get round each corner, along with a turbo speed to go as quick as possible.

Whilst learning about physics and utilising pit stops to maintain both the car and speed.

Genetic Engineering Lab

The player is given different genetic engineering examples of what different animal genes have been tested together.

By looking at what each options the player has to guess which combinations worked.

After each guess, there is extra information about why each combination worked/failed.

Make the Best Fit

Within this game, players must place all the different sized shapes within the area with as little wasted space as possible.

To make the game more challenging there is a time limit for each level with several difficulty levels to choose from, ranging from easy to master.

Each attempt gives the player a percentage score about how much area was used so they can improve on their technique and speed.
